What happens now?

Congratulations, you've made your first move in your first game.

Now, you'll need to wait for the other players in the game to act. When it's your turn to act again, you'll receive an email. After that, if you don't act for 7 days, you'll be timed out and fold. Typically, people make a couple of moves each day in each game.

In the mean time, you can start more games. Just remember, Optimization Mode games split if you split your range — you can easily end up with a dozen concurrent betting lines being played out in different games.

You won't see this message again.
